# Flour_mill_prototype
### ⚙️ Current Situation in Local Mills

- Usually **single employee**
- Only **one order processed at a time**
- No proper system to track:
    - waiting time
    - order queue
- Worker **predicts time manually**, often inaccurate
- During rush:
    - mistakes in **price calculation** can happen
- Transactions are mostly:
    - **hand-to-hand (cash-based)**
    - no digital or written records

---

### ❗ Problems Identified

- Customers don’t know:
    - their position in queue
    - exact waiting time
- Time estimates are:
    - based on guesswork
- Pricing errors:
    - possible due to rush and mental calculation
- No accountability system:
    - owner trusts employee blindly
- No order records:
    - difficult to track daily sales

---

### 💡 Proposed Solution

- A system/machine where:
    - customer order is entered (item + weight)
    - system calculates:
        - price
        - processing time
- System keeps track of:
    - orders
    - queue
- Timer/estimation runs until flour is ready

---

### 🧾 Billing Concept

- System generates an **order bill**
- Bill includes:
    - item
    - weight
    - total price
- Helps:
    - avoid pricing mistakes
    - improve transparency

---

🏪 Business Impact

- Reduces:
    - human calculation errors
    - incorrect time predictions
- Introduces:
    - basic accountability in transactions
- Helps owner:
    - verify employee work
    - track sales
- Improves customer experience:
    - clearer expectations

---

 🧩 Final Vision

- A **dedicated simple machine/device**
    - similar to:
        - shop payment machines
        - bus ticket machines
- Designed specifically for:
    - flour mill usage
- Focus:
    - simplicity
    - fast operation
    - real-world practicality
